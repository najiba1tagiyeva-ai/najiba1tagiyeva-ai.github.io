Azerbaijan’s Path Toward an Energy Transition

This project uses data from 1990–2024 to explore how far Azerbaijan has moved from oil and gas toward renewable energy. The sections below combine energy mix, emissions, investment, geography and regional comparison into one narrative.

1. Introduction

Azerbaijan’s economy has long been shaped by hydrocarbons. As the host of COP29 and a country with growing renewable ambitions, it faces a key question: is it really transitioning to clean energy, or does fossil fuel dependence still dominate? This page provides a data-driven overview of that transition.
